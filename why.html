Why Nature Sounds Help You Relax ?

Nature sounds specifically can help promote relaxation by signifying that it’s time for us to turn off and ease into the element we’re in.

Nature sounds are reminiscent of tranquil environments such as the rain and ocean — sometimes blended with instruments — that come together to craft relaxing nature music
